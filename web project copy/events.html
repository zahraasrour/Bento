Weekly Events

Welcome to our resturant where every wednesday, friday, and sunday an event will be held at 6:00 pm, where anyone can particpate no matter their age, nationality, race or ethenticity. Below you will see a list of the events that will occur with their details. If you would like to join any of these events please click here to make a reservation.

Wednesday Nights :

Cook-off :

The rules are simple :

  1. Contestants aren't allowed to use their own stuff. All the ingredients, utensils, and equipment needed will be provided by the resturant where they will all be the same for all contestants and they will be free to choose what ever they need for their dish.
  2. Everyone has to respect the time limit which is 1 hour, a timer will be set and once it starts all the contestants will start together and once the timer stops everyone has to put their utensils down.
  3. The preparation and completion of anything is to be done within the confines of the cook-off site.
  4. Cooks are to prepare and cook in a sanitary manner as possible. Cooking conditions are subject to inspection by judging committee.

As for the winner he/she will be chosen by the judges, who are the resturants' chefs. The juding criteria is as follows:

  1. Execution.
  2. Appearance.
  3. Taste.

Friday Nights :

Music Competition :
Regarding music competition rules, there are none whatsover. The time limit for the piece that will be presented is 5 minutes. As for the winner, he/she will be chosen depending on the votes of the spectatures .

Sunday Nights :

Mystery :
As the name suggests this event is not known. The winner of the cook-off and that of the music competition will each post an event of their own choice on their social media tagging the resturant. In their post they have to mention all the rules for the event which they will be the judge of. The event that will take place will be chosen depending on which post gets the most amount of likes.
